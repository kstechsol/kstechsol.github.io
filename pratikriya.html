About Pratikriya

Pratikriya is pioneer product of our company, helps in resolving the problem of single platform monitoring of each and every department of any organization.
As the name signifies Pratikriya means the feedback or expressing the feelings and thoughts. By this product the user or the member of any organization can register a complaint, track it and get the latest information or notice about organization by its head and their subordinates. In addition various functionalities are also provided to the user. The organization head can monitor the performance and efficiency of each subordinate by the dashboard available on the organization head panel.
